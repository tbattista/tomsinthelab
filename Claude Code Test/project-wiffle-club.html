Wizards Wiffle Ball Club

  • Category

    Community/Brand Website

  • Platform

    HTML/CSS/JavaScript

  • Live Site

    wizardswiffleball.com

Built a comprehensive branding site and event tracker for the Wizards Wiffle Ball Club, featuring interactive field maps, player statistics, and an invite management system for organizing games and tournaments.

Project Overview

What started as a simple group of friends playing wiffle ball evolved into a full-fledged club that needed proper organization. The website serves as both a public face for the club and a functional tool for managing events, tracking statistics, and coordinating with members.

Key Features

  • Interactive field maps showing game locations
  • Event calendar with RSVP functionality
  • Player profiles and season statistics
  • Photo gallery from games and tournaments
  • Invite management system for organizing games
  • Mobile-responsive design for field-side use
  • Contact forms for new member inquiries

The Fenway Project

One of the coolest aspects was building a scaled replica of Fenway Park in a backyard. The website includes detailed photos of the construction process, field dimensions, and the custom Green Monster we built. This became a centerpiece feature that really showcases the dedication of the club.

The site also includes a virtual tour of the field and information about hosting events at our custom ballpark.

Building a Community Around a Hobby

This project taught me a lot about building for a specific community. The wiffle ball players had very specific needs - they needed to know field conditions, coordinate carpools, and track their stats across different locations. Unlike a generic sports website, this needed to reflect the unique culture of our group.

The most rewarding part was seeing how the website helped grow the club from 8 friends to over 25 regular players. The site became a central hub that new members could reference to understand our traditions, field rules, and upcoming events.

Future updates will include live scoring during games and integration with social media for better photo sharing.
